INSIGHTS OF DASHBOARD:-

-The West region generated the highest total sales, especially during Q3, indicating strong market presence or promotional activity in that area.

-Technology emerged as the top-performing category, contributing the most to overall revenue compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.

-Sales consistently increased during November and December, likely due to holiday season buying patterns, suggesting an opportunity for targeted campaigns.

-The South region showed moderate sales volume but had comparatively lower profits, indicating potential pricing or cost-related inefficiencies.
